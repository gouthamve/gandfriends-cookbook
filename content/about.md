This is a cookbook of recipes contributed by friends. It's deliberately plain text: easy to read while cooking, easy to print, and friendly to slow networks.

Every recipe has an author. Most authors have one or two contributions. Browse by meal/course on the [home page](/), by [author](/authors/), or look something up in the [index](/index/) at the back &mdash; like a real cookbook.

## Contributing

If you'd like to add a recipe, see [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/gouthamve/gnfriends-cookbook/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) in the repository. Recipes are submitted as markdown files via pull request.
